Background


AIC Moiben Theological College

A.I.C Moiben Theological College is an evalengical institution run and managed by a Board of Management appointed by A.I.C Moiben Regional Church Council. It is loacated at Kabobo village, Kapsang Location in Uasin Gishu County and is about 7 kilometers from Kabenes Trading Center.

The college was started in September 2014 and exists to glorify God by providing integrated training for God's servants through character formation and ministry equipment.

Vision

To be an exemplary institution in producing holistical Education for equipping ministers of the Gospel.

Mission

MTC exists to train competent ministers of the gospel through integrated training in order to meet the demands of the church and the contemporary society.

Motto

"Equipping for Harvest"

LUKE 10:2

Aims and Objectives of MTC

  • A.I.C Moiben Theological College exists to teach sound doctrines in an evalengical perspective.
  • M.T.C exists to train and equip church ministers for the ministry.
  • M.T.C exists to develop Lay leaders through seminars and workshops.
