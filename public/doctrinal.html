M.T.C Doctrinal Basis

The beliefs | position of M.T.C is derived from evalengical perspective. The College therefore beilieves in the following:

Unity and Trinity

The Unity and Trinity of God, eternally existing in three co-equal persons, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit.

Creator and Preserver

God the creator and preserver of all things who created man. Male and female in His own image and gave them dominion over the earthly creation.

Diety and Humanity

The diety and humanity of God the Son, the Lord Jesus Christ who being the very God, also became man being begotten of the Holy Spirit, born of Virgin Mary, was crucified, died and buried, was raised and inseparably joined together in one person.

Personality and Necessity

The diety personality of God the Holy Spirit and the necessity of His work to make the death of Christ effective to an individual sinner, granting the sinner repentance towards God in the Lord Jesus Christ and His ministry dwelling permanently within and working through the believer for Godly life and service.

Inspiration and Infallibility

The divine verbal and plenary inspiration and infallibility of the scriptures of the Old and the New Testament as originally given and their absolute and final authority in all matters of faith and practice.

Sinfulness and Guilt

The universall sinfulness and guilt of human nature since the fall and rendering man subject to God's wrath and condemnation.

Representative and Substitute

The sacrificial death of our representative and substitute, the Lord Jesus Christ the incarnate Son of God, by shedding of whose blood atonement was made for the sins of the whole world and where by Him alone are redeemed from guilt, penalty and power of sin and death.

Faith and Salvation

The necessity of the new birth as the work of God the Holy Spirit that men are saved by grace through faith, not by works.

Security and Assurance

The eternal security of the believers, based on the atomic work of the Lord Jesus Christ, whereby was a born-again child of God; He has assurance of salvation and has the right to all pivileges of the Son of God.

Works and Obedience

The maintenance of good works and obedience to the revealed will of God in life and services, through which eternal rewards shall be received.

Head and Members

The true church, whose Head is Lord Jesus Christ whose members are all regenerated persons united to Christ and to one another by baptism of the Holy Spirit.

Baptism and The Lord's Supper

The observance of the ordinances of Baptism and The Lord's Supper as appointed by the Lord Jesus Christ.

Glory and Gospel

The supreme mission of the church as being to Glorify God and preach the Gospel to all nations.

The Physical Return

  • The Rapture
    1 Thessalonians 4:16-17

  • The Tribulation
    Revelation 4:10
    Mathew 24:29

  • The Physical Reign of Christ
    Revelation 20:6

The Resurrection

  • The physical ressurection of all the people.

  • The eternal blessedness of the saved and eternal punishement of the lost.
