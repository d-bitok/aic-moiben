Admissons


Scope

M.T.C accepts all students from Bible believing churches with a condition that they fully respect and abide by the doctrinal beliefs of the college.


Qualification

A diploma prospective student must possess the following qualifications:-

  • Must be Saved, Baptized and of Sound Testimony
  • Must demonstrate that he/she is called of God through involvement in practical ministry e.g. Choir, Sunday school, and Evangelism etc.
  • Must have a K.C.S.E certificate with a mean grade of D+ and above or a certificate from A.I.C training centers.
  • Must be recommended by the Local Church and the District Church Council.

Admission Procedures

  1. Prospective students should apply to admissions office. The forms are available at the college and the Regional office.
  2. Dully filled forms should be accompanied by the following:-
    • Two recent coloured passports of the applicant
    • Copies of :-
      1. National identity card
      2. K.C.S.E certificate
      3. Marriage Certificate if married
      4. Other Academic testimonials
    • Recommendations from LCC and DCC Note:
      Non-A.I.C members must be recommended by the relevant church authority.
    • Financial commitment from the sponsor.
    • Written personal testimony on Salvation, Christian life and calling into the ministry.
    • Application fee of Kshs.500/= (Non-refundable)

Mature Entry

This programme is designed for those who have a call but do not meet the required qualification. The program targets those who have the following:

  1. K.C.P.E Certificate
  2. Proven capability and experience in the ministry
  3. Training Backgrounds e.g. Certificate from A.I.C Training Centres.
